üìã Installation Instructions

  1. Get Your Tracking Token
    • Log in to the Asset Management System
    • Go to "Device Tracking" page (Admin/Manager only)
    • Click "Enable Tracking" and select your device
    • Copy the tracking token shown
  2. Download Installer
    • Click the appropriate button above for your OS
    • Save the installer to your computer
  3. Run Installer
    • Windows: Open PowerShell as Administrator and run:
      .\install.ps1 -ServerUrl "https://YOUR-SERVER" -TrackingToken "TOKEN"
    • Linux/macOS: Open Terminal and run:
      sudo ./install.sh --server-url "https://YOUR-SERVER" --tracking-token "TOKEN"
  4. Verify Installation
    • Wait 5 minutes for the first heartbeat
    • Check Device Tracking page - device should show as "Online"
